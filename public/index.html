FAQ

Common questions.

Which TVs are supported? +
Any Android TV running Android 8.0 or later. This includes popular brands like Mi, Sony Bravia (Android TV), TCL, OnePlus TV, and more. Fire TV and Tizen are not currently supported natively.
How do I install the app on my TV? +
Enable "Install from unknown sources" in your TV settings, then sideload via a USB drive, or use apps like Downloader to install directly from our secure URL. A comprehensive video guide is included.
What is your refund policy? +
We offer a 7-day no-questions-asked refund guarantee. If the software doesn't work perfectly on your setup and our support team can't resolve it, you receive a full refund instantly.
How does the activation process work? +
Following your purchase, you'll receive a unique license key via email. Open the app, select "Activate", paste your key, and you're good to go. Internet is only required for the initial activation step.
Which LED controllers are compatible? +
We support WLED (via WiFi/USB), Govee (via local API), and any ESP8266/ESP32-based custom controller. A detailed wiring guide and WLED firmware flash instructions are included in the welcome package.
