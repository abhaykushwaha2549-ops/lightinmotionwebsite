Privacy Policy

Last Updated — June 2026

LIGHTINMOTION ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the LIGHTINMOTION mobile application collects, uses, and protects information when you use our services. The application is designed to operate primarily on your local network and does not require account creation.

Information We Collect

Information We Collect

LIGHTINMOTION is designed to operate primarily on your local network and does not require account creation. The application may access the following device features to provide its core functionality.

Camera Access

The camera is used exclusively for scanning QR codes to connect compatible devices such as WLED controllers and LIGHTINMOTION TV devices. No images or video are stored or transmitted.

Microphone Access

The microphone may be used for Music Sync features that allow lighting effects to react to ambient audio captured by the device. Audio data is processed in real-time on your device only and is never stored or transmitted to our servers.

Screen Capture

With your explicit permission, the application may capture screen content for Mobile Sync and TV Sync features. Screen data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers at any time.

Local Network Access

The application scans your local network to discover and connect to compatible devices including WLED controllers and LIGHTINMOTION TV devices. Network discovery is limited to your local environment and no network data is transmitted externally.

How We Use & Store Your Data

How We Use Information

The information accessed by the application is used solely to deliver core functionality:

  • Discover and connect compatible devices on your local network
  • Enable real-time lighting synchronization
  • Provide Music Sync functionality
  • Enable Mobile Sync and TV Sync features
  • Improve application stability and performance

Data Storage

LIGHTINMOTION stores configuration information locally on your device only. This includes:

  • Device connection settings
  • Lighting layouts and scene configurations
  • Calibration settings
  • User preferences and app state
Sharing & Security

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties. The application may communicate directly with devices on your local network. No personal information is transmitted to external third-party services unless explicitly stated within the application.

Security

We implement reasonable technical measures to protect information processed by the application. However, no electronic transmission or storage method is completely secure. We encourage users to keep their device software updated to maintain the highest level of protection.

Your Rights & Contact

Children's Privacy

LIGHTINMOTION is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or process information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us immediately.

User Rights

You may revoke permissions such as Camera, Microphone, or Screen Capture at any time through your device settings. Revoking a permission will disable the features that depend on it, but will not affect other application functionality.

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the application constitutes acceptance of any updated policy.
